# Daily Review

Morning planning pass. Five to ten minutes.

## Trigger

- Manual: "Run my daily review"
- Scheduled: every weekday at 8am via [[02_Capabilities/skills/schedule]]

## Steps

**1. Pull state.** Read:
   - [[01_Identity/context]] — what's on Vladimir's mind right now
   - [[04_Projects/README]] — active projects
   - [[07_Inbox/README]] — captures from yesterday
   - Calendar (if connector available) — today's meetings

**2. Pick the day's focus.** Three things, max. State them as outcomes, not activities.

**3. Pre-process the inbox.** Anything that takes <2 minutes to file or respond → do it. Larger items → triage to a project or a clear next step.

**4. Surface anything blocking.** "X is blocked on Y from Sarah" → flag for follow-up, don't bury.

**5. Output.** A short note in `06_Memory/episodes/<date>-daily.md` (use [[99_Templates/daily-note]]) with:
   - Today's three
   - Anything carried over from yesterday
   - Any flag/blocker

## Verification step

Re-read the three. Are they outcomes? "Reply to Sarah" is fine. "Work on the report" is not — what's the outcome? "Draft section 2 of the report" works.
