# Quick Lookup

Fast factual answer. One or two queries, direct response, no synthesis ceremony.

## Trigger

- "What is X?"
- "When did Y happen?"
- "Who's the current Z?"
- Any single-fact question

## Steps

1. If the answer is in stable training knowledge AND the cost of being wrong is low → answer directly.
2. If the topic could have changed since the cutoff (May 2025) OR the cost of being wrong is real → search.
3. State the answer plainly. Cite the source if searched.

## When to escalate to deep research

- The question has multiple plausible answers
- The answer depends on conditions not yet specified
- Vladimir asks a follow-up — that signals there's more to explore
