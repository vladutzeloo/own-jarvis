# Meeting Prep

Get ready for a specific meeting. 5-15 minutes depending on stakes.

## Trigger

- "Prep me for my meeting with X"
- "What do I need to know for the Y review"

## Steps

**1. Pull the meeting context.**
   - From calendar (if available): time, attendees, prior notes
   - From email/Slack (if available): recent threads with attendees
   - From [[06_Memory/README]]: any prior decisions or episodes involving these people

**2. Identify the decision or outcome.** What does Vladimir want to be true after this meeting? If "no decision needed, just an update", say so explicitly — that changes the prep.

**3. Build the prep brief.**
   - Who's in the room and what they care about
   - The 2-3 things that matter for the outcome
   - One paragraph of background (in case Vladimir is rusty)
   - Any open questions Vladimir should bring

**4. Save the brief.** As `06_Memory/episodes/<date>-prep-<meeting>.md`. Reference any project files involved.

## After the meeting

Pair this with a meeting-notes file (use [[99_Templates/meeting-notes]]). Capture: what was decided, what's owned by whom, when to check in. File anything decision-shaped to `06_Memory/decisions/`.
