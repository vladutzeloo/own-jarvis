# Draft Email

Write an email in Vladimir's voice. Not for sending — for drafting that he'll review and send himself.

## Trigger

- "Write an email to X about Y"
- "Reply to this thread"

## Steps

**1. Read [[01_Identity/preferences]].** Tone is direct, warm, no hedging. Match that.

**2. Identify the goal.** Information transfer? Request? Pushback? Apology? The structure differs.

**3. Lead with the point.** Subject line states the matter. First sentence delivers the goal. Detail and context come after.

**4. Draft.** Keep it short. Default length: three short paragraphs. Long emails are rarely better than short ones.

**5. Sign off appropriately.** Vladimir's standard sign-off is `Vladimir`. (Update if preference changes.)

**6. Surface the draft.** Put it in chat or in a markdown file in `07_Inbox/` — Vladimir reviews and sends himself.

## Common patterns

- **Request:** subject states the ask, first line restates it, second line gives context, third line gives a date or next step.
- **Pushback:** acknowledge their point, state the disagreement, propose the path forward. Don't sandwich with niceties.
- **Update:** what's done, what's next, what you need.
